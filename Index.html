About me


My senior project

My name is Aryashree and I am a senior at Lincoln School. I have been at Lincoln Since 8th grade. I like to play sports, video games and also I like to cook alot so that is what I based this project on. For this project, I will be showcasing my recipes through a website that I will be coding. This will show both my personal life and my school life as cooking is something I do for fun and coding is somthing I wish to do for college. While I like doing both I was never able to connect both of them together. I never saw how combining cooking and coding was possible. While there are things like making machines and coding them, that was at an industrial grade and not something I could accomplice. So for a much smaller grade, I decided to code a website where I could share my recipes with other people. I wanted to understand if it was possible for someone to combine the 2 and enjoy the process at the same time. This project will be a challenge and will help me grow as a lerner because I have never coded a website before. This project will also help me understand how I can make my recipes different than others.
